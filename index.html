Academic Support

Find answers to your academic queries, access study materials, and get information about your curriculum.

Where can I find the syllabus for my class?
The complete syllabus for all classes is available in the school library. You can also request a digital copy from your class teacher or subject incharge.
How can I get study materials for competitive exams?
The school has a dedicated section in the library for competitive exam preparation. You can also approach your subject teachers for guidance and recommended resources.
What is the procedure for changing subjects in class 11th?
Subject changes are only permitted within the first month of the academic session. You need to submit a written application to the principal through your class teacher, with valid reasons and parent consent.

When will the half-yearly exam results be declared?
Half-yearly exam results are typically declared within 15 days after the completion of exams. The exact date will be announced by the examination department.
What documents do I need for collecting my mark sheet?
You need to bring your school ID card and a copy of the hall ticket. If collecting on behalf of someone else, you need an authorization letter from the student.

Admission Procedures

Information about admission process, eligibility, and required documents.

What is the admission process for class 11th?
Admissions for class 11th are based on class 10th board exam results. The process typically begins in May. You need to submit the application form along with required documents and fee.
What documents are required for new admission?
Required documents include: Transfer Certificate, Birth Certificate, Aadhar Card, Previous Class Mark Sheet, Caste Certificate (if applicable), and passport size photographs.
Is there any reservation policy for admissions?
Yes, the school follows government reservation policies. Specific details about reservation percentages can be obtained from the admission office.

How can I join the school cricket team?
Selection trials for the cricket team are held at the beginning of each academic year. Keep an eye on announcements for trial dates. You can also approach the sports teacher for more information.
Are there any sports scholarships available?
Yes, the school offers sports scholarships for exceptional performers. Details can be obtained from the sports department.

What are the benefits of joining NCC?
NCC provides leadership training, discipline, adventure activities, and opportunities for personality development. It also offers certificate programs that can be beneficial for career opportunities.
Is NCC compulsory for all students?
No, NCC is a voluntary activity. Students from class 8th onwards can choose to enroll in NCC.
